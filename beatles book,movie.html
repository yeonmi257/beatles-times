The Beatles: Books & Movies

5 Great Books About The Beatles

The Beatles Anthology

Author: The Beatles

The official autobiography written by The Beatles themselves. Rich in photos and personal stories.

Revolution in the Head

Author: Ian MacDonald

An in-depth track-by-track analysis of every Beatles song. Critical and insightful.

Tune In

Author: Mark Lewisohn

The definitive biography of the early Beatles years. First volume in a planned trilogy.

The Beatles: The Biography

Author: Bob Spitz

A comprehensive and narrative-driven biography of the band from childhood to breakup.

With The Beatles

Author: Patrick Humphries

Rich with exclusive interviews and rare perspectives on The Beatles’ rise to fame.

5 Must-Watch Films About The Beatles

Beatles '64

Director: Martin Scorsese

Year: 2024

A new documentary about their 1964 US tour. Emotional, stylish, and powerful.

Let It Be

Director: Michael Lindsay-Hogg

Year: 1970

A classic documentary capturing the tensions and brilliance during their final days.

A Hard Day's Night

Director: Richard Lester

Year: 1964

The Beatles’ debut film, part mockumentary, part musical — a pop culture landmark.

Help!

Director: Richard Lester

Year: 1965

Colorful, surreal adventure film starring The Beatles. Hilarious and chaotic.

The Beatles: Get Back

Director: Peter Jackson

Year: 2021

An intimate look into their final sessions, meticulously restored from original footage.
