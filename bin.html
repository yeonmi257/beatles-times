Liverpool, Monday May 27, 2025

The Beatles Incident News

Chronicle of Moments That Changed History

By Seungbin Park

July 15, 1958

John Lennon's Mother Dies

Julia Lennon died in a car accident, which deeply impacted young John and influenced later songs like "Julia".

August 17, 1960

The Beatles Begin First Hamburg Shows

Their time in Hamburg was filled with tough conditions, arrests, and various incidents during club performances.

March 4, 1966

“More Popular Than Jesus”

John Lennon’s comment led to protests and record burnings in the U.S. after being reprinted in Datebook.

August 27, 1967

Brian Epstein Dies

The Beatles’ manager passed away from an overdose, leaving the group with uncertainty and shifting leadership.

April 10, 1970

Breakup Announcement

Paul McCartney confirmed The Beatles’ breakup, shocking fans and the press across the globe.

December 8, 1980

John Lennon Assassinated

Mark David Chapman shot Lennon outside the Dakota Building in NYC, sending shockwaves worldwide.

November 29, 2001

George Harrison Dies After Cancer Battle (Age 58)

George passed away after a long battle with cancer, leaving fans heartbroken and in mourning.

November 2, 2023

Final Beatles Song Released

“Now and Then” completed with AI using Lennon’s demo, marks the official last song by the band.
